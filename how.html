How It Works

Foodie Roulette makes deciding where to go fast and fun with a simple four-step flow.

1 — Tell Us Your Vibe

A quick onboarding quiz learns your food personality — cuisine, mood, and budget.

2 — Discover or Decide

Browse the live map, or let Quick Pick and the Spin Wheel choose for you instantly.

3 — Check In & Enjoy

Tap to check in, read reviews, and use Bar Roulette to keep the group moving together.

4 — Wrap Up Safely

DD Mode and the Drink Tracker keep the night responsible, with a clean summary after.
