Core Features

Every night out, simplified.

No more group chat debates or endless scrolling. Foodie Roulette gives you all the tools to discover, decide, and enjoy the night—all in one app.

01
Quick Pick & Spin Now

Set your search radius and food type, tap "Spin Now", and let the wheel decide. Filter by specific cuisines, price ranges, or venue types.

02
Bar Roulette

Create a bar crawl, invite your friends, and let the app decide the next stop. Built-in Challenges keep the night moving and everyone involved.

03
Discover & Venue Details

Browse restaurants, bars, and street food vendors on a live map. Tap any venue to see photos, menus, check-ins, and verified community reviews.

04
Drink Tracker & DD Mode

Track how many drinks everyone in the group has had. Designate a designated driver (DD) in one tap, and get a clear Night Summary so no one drives unsafely.

05
Community Reviews

Rate your experience with tags, photos, and quick reviews. Read what others actually said about food, ambiance, and service before you even step inside.

06
Onboarding Questionnaire

A quick quiz learns your cuisine preferences, budget range, and dining mood. Whether you're feeling adventurous or craving something specific, we start recommending what fits you.

How It Works

From "where?" to checked-in in 4 steps.

Foodie Roulette handles everything—from deciding where to go, to tracking drinks and safely wrapping up your night out.

1
Tell Us Your Vibe

Complete the Onboarding Questionnaire so we know your cuisine, budget, and mood. Or, use the live map to search on your own.

2
Spin & Decide

Can't agree? Tap Quick Pick or use the Spin Now wheel. The app chooses a venue for you based on your filters and current location.

3
Check-In & Bar Crawl

Tap to Check-In at your venue. If you're out with friends, activate Bar Roulette to decide the next stop and complete built-in challenges.

4
Track & Wrap Up Safely

Use the Drink Tracker and DD Mode throughout the night. At the end, check your Night Summary and leave a review for the community.
