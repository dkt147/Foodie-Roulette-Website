All Features

Everything You Need
for a Great Night

Foodie Roulette isn't just a discovery app — it's a complete toolkit for finding the spot, deciding fast, and having a great (and responsible) time.

Discover Nearby Venues

Restaurants, bars, and street food vendors — all surfaced in one unified live map at any destination. No destination is off-limits.

  • Restaurants, bars & street vendors
  • Live filters: cuisine, price, open now
  • Works in any city

Quick Pick & Spin the Wheel

Can't decide? Set your radius, cuisine, and budget, then spin the wheel — Foodie Roulette picks the venue for you, directly eliminating the group debate.

  • Customizable radius & filters
  • Instant decision in seconds
  • No more "I don't know, you pick"

Bar Roulette

Create a crawl, invite your friends, and let the app choose the next stop. Built-in challenges keep the night moving and everyone involved.

  • Group crawls with joined players
  • Built-in challenges
  • Works for any size group

Drink Tracker & DD Mode

Track drinks per person through the night and designate a driver in one tap — drivers always know who's safe to drive, with a clean summary the next morning.

  • Per-person drink tracking
  • One-tap designated driver
  • Night summary recap

Ratings & Community Reviews

Rate what stood out, write a quick review, and read community-verified reviews from real check-ins — know what's actually good before you even leave.

  • Check-in verified reviews
  • Quick star + comment flow
  • Share your finds with friends

Food Personality Quiz

Foodie Roulette learns your taste from a short onboarding quiz — cuisine, comfort vs. adventurous, budget — so every recommendation actually fits you.

  • One-minute onboarding quiz
  • Personalized recommendations
  • Gets smarter with every visit
